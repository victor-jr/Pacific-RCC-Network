Pacific RCC Network

Climate Monitoring Node

This is the Home of the Climate Monitoring Node for the WMO RA-V Pacific Regional Climate Centre (RCC) Network. It serves as a gateway to the RA VPacific RCC Network as well as a broad range of information related to climate in the Pacific Islands.

A more extensive set of links can be found under the Climate Monitoring Function of the PRCC

The RA V Pacific RCC Network in its initial phase consists of five nodes. Each node is composed of a consortium led by a lead instituition or co-leads. The University of Hawaii (UH) and the US National Oceanographic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) serve as co-leads for the Climate Monitoring node.
